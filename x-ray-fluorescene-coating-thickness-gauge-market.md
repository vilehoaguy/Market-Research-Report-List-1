<p><p>The X-ray Fluorescence (XRF) Coating Thickness Gauge is a sophisticated analytical instrument used to measure the thickness of coatings, such as paint or plating, on substrates without damaging the surface. This non-destructive method leverages the principle of X-ray fluorescence, where X-rays excite the atoms in the coating, causing them to emit secondary X-rays.
