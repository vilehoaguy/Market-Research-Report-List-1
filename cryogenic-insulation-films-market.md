<p><p>Cryogenic insulation films are specialized materials designed to minimize heat transfer in environments with extremely low temperatures. The market is categorized into two types: single side coated and double side coated films. Single side coated films feature an insulating layer on one side, suitable for applications where only one surface requires protection. In contrast, double side coated films offer insulation on both sides, providing enhanced thermal performance and durability, making them ideal for more demanding cryogenic applications such as storage tanks and pipelines.</p></p>
