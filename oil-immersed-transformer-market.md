<p><p>The oil-immersed transformer market primarily consists of two types of transformer oils: paraffin-based and naphtha-based. Paraffin-based transformer oil is derived from refined mineral oils, offering high electrical insulation properties and thermal stability, making it suitable for various applications. Naphtha-based transformer oil, on the other hand, is derived from petroleum distillates and is often used in specialized applications. Both oil types are crucial for efficient transformer operation, ensuring reliable performance and longevity in electrical systems.</p></p>
