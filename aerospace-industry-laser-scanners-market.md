<p><p>The aerospace industry utilizes laser scanners primarily in two market types: non-contact and contact types. Non-contact laser scanners employ laser beams to gather precise measurements without physical interaction, making them ideal for delicate components and large surfaces. In contrast, contact laser scanners require physical contact to measure objects, ensuring high accuracy for small parts and intricate geometries. Both types enhance quality control, streamline manufacturing processes, and facilitate reverse engineering, contributing to improved efficiency and precision in aerospace applications.</p></p>
