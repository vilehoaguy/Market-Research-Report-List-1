<p><ul><li>Monofocal Lenses</li><li>Bifocal Lenses</li><li>Trifocal Lenses</li><li>Progressive Multifocal Lenses</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lens market consists of various types catering to different vision needs. Monofocal lenses offer a single focal point, ideal for those requiring vision correction for one distance. Bifocal lenses feature two distinct sections for distance and near vision. Trifocal lenses incorporate three segments for distance, intermediate, and near vision. Progressive multifocal lenses provide a smooth transition between multiple focal points without visible lines. Other specialty lenses include occupational and photochromic lenses, addressing specific lifestyle requirements and visual preferences.</p></p>
