<p><p>The lipase testing reagent market includes methods such as the turbidity method and the colorimetric method. The turbidity method measures the cloudiness of a solution caused by lipase activity, providing a quantitative assessment of enzyme levels. The colorimetric method, on the other hand, utilizes specific reagents that produce a color change in response to lipase activity, allowing for precise measurements. Both methods are essential in clinical diagnostics, enabling healthcare professionals to evaluate pancreatic function and diagnose related disorders effectively.</p></p>
