<p><ul><li>Open Cell Foam</li><li>Closed Cell Foam</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The filtration foams market is divided into two primary types: open cell foam and closed cell foam. Open cell foam has interconnected pores, allowing air and liquids to flow freely, making it ideal for applications requiring high permeability and ease of filtration. In contrast, closed cell foam features tightly packed cells that provide excellent insulation and resistance to moisture, making it suitable for environments where durability and protection are critical. Both types serve essential roles in various industrial and consumer applications.</p></p>
