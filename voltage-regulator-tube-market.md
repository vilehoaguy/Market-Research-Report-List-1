<p><strong>The Voltage-regulator Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ferroresonant</li><li>Tap Switching</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The voltage-regulator tube market includes two primary types: Ferroresonant and Tap Switching regulators. Ferroresonant regulators utilize magnetic components to maintain steady output voltage despite input fluctuations, offering excellent voltage stability and overload protection. They are ideal for applications requiring consistent performance. Tap Switching regulators, on the other hand, adjust voltage levels by switching between preset taps on a transformer, providing flexibility and efficiency. Both types serve critical roles in ensuring reliable power supply across various industrial and commercial sectors.</p></p>
