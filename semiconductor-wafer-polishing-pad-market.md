<p><ul><li>Hard CMP Pads</li><li>Soft CMP Pads</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor wafer polishing pad market is divided into two main types: hard CMP pads and soft CMP pads. Hard CMP pads are designed for precise material removal and are typically used in advanced semiconductor fabrication processes, offering durability and longevity. In contrast, soft CMP pads provide greater conformability and are ideal for delivering smoother finishes, making them suitable for polishing delicate substrates. Both types play crucial roles in achieving the required surface quality and flatness in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
