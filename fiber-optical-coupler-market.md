<p><p>The fiber optical coupler market features several types, each serving specific functions. Y Couplers split optical signals into two outputs, ideal for simple distribution. T Couplers connect multiple fibers, allowing for streamlined interconnections. Star Couplers provide a centralized connection for numerous fibers, enhancing signal distribution in networks. Tree Couplers, resembling a branching structure, facilitate connections in hierarchical configurations, making them suitable for complex systems. Each type caters to diverse networking needs, driving innovation in fiber optics technology.</p></p>
