<p><p>Cardiac Rhythm Management (CRM) devices encompass various technologies designed to monitor and regulate heart rhythms. Pacemakers are implanted to maintain a regular heartbeat in patients with bradycardia. Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators (ICDs) help prevent sudden cardiac death by monitoring the heart and delivering shocks for life-threatening arrhythmias. Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy (CRT) devices improve heart efficiency by coordinating contractions in patients with heart failure. Together, these devices enhance patient outcomes and quality of life through effective rhythm management.</p></p>
