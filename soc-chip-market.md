<p><p>The SoC (System on Chip) market can be categorized into three main types: MSI (Medium Scale Integration), LSI (Large Scale Integration), and VLSI (Very Large Scale Integration). MSI integrates hundreds of transistors onto a single chip, suitable for basic applications. LSI encompasses thousands of transistors, enabling more complex functionalities. VLSI takes this further, integrating millions of transistors, allowing for highly sophisticated computing power and advanced applications, making it prevalent in smartphones, advanced computing devices, and IoT technologies.</p></p>
