<p><p>The 128-slice CT market is divided into two main types: Spiral CT and Conventional CT. Spiral CT, also known as helical CT, utilizes continuous rotation of the X-ray tube and a constant patient movement through the scanner, producing high-resolution images quickly, making it ideal for complex diagnostics. In contrast, Conventional CT involves stationary imaging with sequential exposures, leading to longer scan times and lower image quality in comparison. Both types serve various clinical applications depending on the required speed and detail.</p></p>
