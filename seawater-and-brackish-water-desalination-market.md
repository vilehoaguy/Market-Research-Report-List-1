<p><ul><li>Multistage Flash Distillation</li><li>Multi-Effect Distillation</li><li>Vapor Compression</li><li>Reverse Osmosis</li><li>Nanofiltration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The seawater and brackish water desalination market includes several key technologies: </p><p>1. **Multistage Flash Distillation (MSF)** uses multiple stages of heating and cooling to evaporate and condense seawater, maximizing efficiency. </p><p>2. **Multi-Effect Distillation (MED)** employs multiple chambers to further exploit the heat generated. </p><p>3. **Vapor Compression (VC)** uses mechanical energy to compress vapor, facilitating condensation. </p><p>4. **Reverse Osmosis (RO)** forces water through semi-permeable membranes, effectively removing salts. </p><p>5. **Nanofiltration (NF)** utilizes membranes to separate larger ions, offering a selective approach to desalination.</p></p>
