<p><strong>The Flexible Alternating Current Transmission Systems (FACTS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shunt Compensation</li><li>Series Compensation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flexible Alternating Current Transmission Systems (FACTS) enhance the control and stability of AC transmission networks. The market for FACTS can be categorized into shunt compensation and series compensation. Shunt compensation involves reactive power support to improve voltage profiles and reduce transmission losses. In contrast, series compensation works by adjusting line impedance, increasing power transfer capability, and stabilizing system performance. Both technologies play crucial roles in optimizing the efficiency and reliability of power transmission systems globally.</p></p>
