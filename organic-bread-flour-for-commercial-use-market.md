<p><p>Organic bread flour for commercial use can be categorized into two main types based on milling techniques: machine milled flour and stone ground flour. Machine milled flour is produced using industrial equipment that ensures uniformity and increased production efficiency, making it suitable for large-scale baking operations. In contrast, stone ground flour is milled using traditional stones, preserving the nutrition and flavor of the grains, offering a coarser texture. Both types cater to different baking preferences and product quality demands in the market.</p></p>
