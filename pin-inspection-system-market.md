<p><p>The Pin Inspection System market comprises two primary types: 2D Pin Inspection Systems and 3D Pin Inspection Systems. </p><p>2D Pin Inspection Systems utilize two-dimensional imaging technology to assess the accuracy of pins on circuit boards, focusing on positional verification and dimensional measurement. </p><p>In contrast, 3D Pin Inspection Systems offer a three-dimensional view, enabling detailed analysis of pin placement, height, and overall geometry, which enhances detection of defects and ensures higher quality control in manufacturing processes. Both systems are essential for maintaining product reliability.</p></p>
