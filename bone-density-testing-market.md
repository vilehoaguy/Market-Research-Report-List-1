<p><p>The bone density testing market includes various types of technologies. Quantitative ultrasound (QUS) uses sound waves to measure bone density, offering a portable and radiation-free option. Peripheral quantitative computed tomography (pQCT) provides detailed 3D images of bone structure and density, allowing for in-depth analysis beyond traditional methods. Other methods may include dual-energy X-ray absorptiometry (DEXA) and other advanced imaging techniques. Each type serves distinct clinical needs, contributing to the overall assessment and monitoring of bone health.</p></p>
