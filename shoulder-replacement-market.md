<p><strong>The Shoulder Replacement Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anatomical Shoulder Prosthesis</li><li>Reverse Shoulder Prosthesis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The shoulder replacement market primarily features two types of prostheses: anatomical shoulder prostheses and reverse shoulder prostheses. Anatomical shoulder prostheses mimic the natural structure of the shoulder, preserving the original anatomy for better mobility and function, typically used in patients with arthritis or fractures. In contrast, reverse shoulder prostheses reverse the joint's design, relocating the ball and socket components to improve stability and function, especially beneficial for patients with rotator cuff tears and complex shoulder issues. Both types aim to enhance patient outcomes and quality of life.</p></p>
