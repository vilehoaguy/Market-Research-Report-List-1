<p><p>The USB joystick market is primarily divided into two types: single-axis and multi-axis joysticks. Single-axis joysticks offer control along a single direction, making them suitable for specific applications like throttle control in simulators. In contrast, multi-axis joysticks provide comprehensive movement capabilities across multiple directions, enabling more complex interactions and gaming experiences. This versatility makes multi-axis joysticks popular in gaming, virtual reality, and flight simulation, while single-axis versions find niche applications in various industries.</p></p>
