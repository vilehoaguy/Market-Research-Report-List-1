<p><p>The piezoelectric motors and actuators market comprises three main material types: ceramics, composites, and polymers. Ceramic piezoelectric materials, such as lead zirconate titanate (PZT), are known for their high efficiency and stability, making them popular in precise applications. Composites combine the benefits of ceramics and polymers, enhancing performance while reducing weight. Polymer-based piezoelectric materials offer flexibility and ease of fabrication, attracting applications in soft robotics and miniaturized devices. Each type caters to varying demands across industries.</p></p>
