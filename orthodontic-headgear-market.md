<p><strong>The Orthodontic Headgear Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cervical Pull</li><li>High-Pull Headgear</li><li>Reverse-Pull (Facemask)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Orthodontic headgear is designed to correct dental and jaw alignment issues. The cervical pull headgear uses straps anchored to the back of the head, guiding molars backward. High-pull headgear connects to the upper jaw and pulls the teeth upward, suitable for overbite cases. Reverse-pull (facemask) headgear pulls the upper jaw forward, helping treat underbites. Each type targets specific alignment challenges, enhancing the effectiveness of orthodontic treatment and contributing to overall dental health.</p></p>
