<p><p>The High Purity Adipic Acid market comprises three primary production methods: Cyclohexane Oxidation, Cyclohexene Oxidation, and Phenol Hydrogenation. Cyclohexane Oxidation involves the air oxidation of cyclohexane to produce adipic acid, while Cyclohexene Oxidation uses cyclohexene as a more reactive substrate for higher yields. Phenol Hydrogenation transforms phenol into adipic acid through hydrogenation reactions. Each method impacts the quality and purity of the adipic acid produced, catering to diverse industrial applications and consumer requirements.</p></p>
