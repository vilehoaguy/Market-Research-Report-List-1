<p><p>Energy Recovery Devices (ERDs) are essential for enhancing energy efficiency in industrial processes. Reverse turbines convert pressure energy from fluids into mechanical energy. Pelton wheels harness kinetic energy from water by using spoon-shaped buckets. Pressure exchangers transfer energy between fluids at different pressures, optimizing resource use. Work exchangers minimize energy loss by transferring work between fluids. Electric motor drives utilize electricity to drive operations efficiently, promoting sustainability. Together, these technologies contribute to reducing energy waste and boosting operational efficiency.</p></p>
