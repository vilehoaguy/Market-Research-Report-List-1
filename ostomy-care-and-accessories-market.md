<p><p>The ostomy care and accessories market is primarily divided into two types: one-piece and traditional two-piece ostomy products. One-piece ostomy products integrate the pouch and skin barrier into a single system, simplifying application and maintenance, making them user-friendly. In contrast, traditional two-piece ostomy products consist of a separate skin barrier and pouch, allowing customization and ease of changing the pouch without replacing the skin barrier. Both types cater to diverse user preferences and needs in ostomy care.</p></p>
