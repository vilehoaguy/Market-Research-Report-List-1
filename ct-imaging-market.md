<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The CT Imaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>X-ray CT</li><li>Positron Emission Tomography (PET)</li><li>Single-photon Emission Computed Tomography (SPECT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CT imaging market comprises several key types, each specializing in distinct imaging techniques. X-ray CT uses X-rays to create detailed cross-sectional images of the body, primarily for diagnosing various conditions. Positron Emission Tomography (PET) utilizes radioactive tracers to visualize metabolic processes, aiding in cancer detection and neurological assessments. Single-photon Emission Computed Tomography (SPECT) produces 3D images by detecting gamma rays, often used in cardiology and oncology. Together, these technologies enhance diagnostic capabilities in medical imaging.</p></p>
