<p><p>Automatic espresso machines are categorized into two primary types: semi-automatic and fully-automatic. Semi-automatic machines give users control over the brewing process, allowing them to manage water flow and extraction time, while still automating certain tasks like water heating and pressure regulation. Fully-automatic machines streamline the entire process, offering one-touch operation for brewing, frothing, and dispensing espresso, making them ideal for convenience and ease of use. Each type caters to different user preferences, from coffee enthusiasts to busy individuals seeking quick solutions.</p></p>
