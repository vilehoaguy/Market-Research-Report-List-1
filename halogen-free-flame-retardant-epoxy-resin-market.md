<p><ul><li>Reactive Type Epoxy Resin</li><li>Additive Type Epoxy Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The halogen-free flame retardant epoxy resin market is categorized into two main types: reactive type epoxy resin and additive type epoxy resin. Reactive type epoxy resin incorporates flame retardant agents into its chemical structure, enhancing its thermal stability and fire resistance during the curing process. In contrast, additive type epoxy resin involves adding flame retardant materials to the resin formulation, which provides fire resistance without altering the resin's primary characteristics. Both types cater to the increasing demand for safer, environmentally friendly materials.</p></p>
