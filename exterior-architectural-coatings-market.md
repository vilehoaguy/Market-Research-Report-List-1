<p><strong>The Exterior Architectural Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-Borne</li><li>Water-Borne</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Exterior architectural coatings can be categorized into solvent-borne and water-borne types. Solvent-borne coatings are primarily made with organic solvents, offering superior durability and protection against harsh weather conditions, but may emit higher levels of volatile organic compounds (VOCs). In contrast, water-borne coatings utilize water as the main solvent, making them more environmentally friendly with lower VOC emissions. They provide ease of application, quick drying times, and good adhesion while being suitable for various substrates in exterior applications.</p></p>
