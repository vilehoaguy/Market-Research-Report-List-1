<p><strong>The EPDM Rubber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Modified Ethylene Propylene Rubber</li><li>Thermoplastic Ethylene-Propylene Rubber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The EPDM rubber market comprises two primary types: Modified Ethylene Propylene Rubber (m-EPDM) and Thermoplastic Ethylene-Propylene Rubber (TP-EPDM). m-EPDM is enhanced for improved performance in specific applications, offering better resistance and flexibility, making it suitable for automotive and construction industries. TP-EPDM, on the other hand, combines the properties of thermoplastics and elastomers, enabling easy processing and recyclability, which is advantageous for various end-use applications like seals and gaskets. Both types cater to diverse market demands.</p></p>
