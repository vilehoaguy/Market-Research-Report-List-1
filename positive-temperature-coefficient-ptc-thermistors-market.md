<p><p>Positive Temperature Coefficient (PTC) thermistors are temperature-sensitive devices that exhibit an increase in resistance with rising temperature. Two main market types are Conductive PTC (CPTC) and Polymer PTC (PPTC). CPTCs are typically ceramic-based and are used for applications like overcurrent protection and temperature sensing, while PPTCs, made from polymer materials, are commonly found in electronics for resettable fuses, providing effective circuit protection. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing safety and reliability in various electronic devices.</p></p>
