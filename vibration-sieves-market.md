<p><p>Vibration sieves are classified into horizontal and vertical types, each serving specific applications in material separation. Horizontal vibration sieves operate parallel to the ground, effectively handling larger volumes of material and allowing for efficient sorting. In contrast, vertical vibration sieves are oriented upright, promoting faster separation in tighter spaces, making them ideal for fine particle processing. Both types utilize vibrations to enhance the screening process, adapting to various industries such as construction, food, pharmaceuticals, and mining.</p></p>
