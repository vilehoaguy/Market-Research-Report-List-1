<p><p>The road traffic coating market encompasses various types including paint, thermoplastic, preformed polymer tape, and epoxy. Paint is widely used for road markings due to its quick application and cost-effectiveness. Thermoplastic is a durable, heat-applied material that provides excellent visibility and longevity. Preformed polymer tape offers easy installation and immediate application, ideal for intricate designs. Epoxy provides strong adhesion and chemical resistance, making it suitable for high-traffic areas. Each type serves specific needs in enhancing road safety and visibility.</p></p>
