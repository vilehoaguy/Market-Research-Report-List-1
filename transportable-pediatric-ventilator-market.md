<p><strong>The Transportable Pediatric Ventilator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intrusive</li><li>Non-invasive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Transportable Pediatric Ventilator Market is categorized into intrusive and non-invasive types. Intrusive ventilators involve the use of endotracheal tubes to maintain airway support, making them suitable for critically ill children requiring intensive respiratory assistance. In contrast, non-invasive ventilators use masks or nasal interfaces to deliver support without intubation, making them more comfortable for patients with less severe conditions. Both types are portable, catering to the specific needs of pediatric patients, ensuring flexibility in various healthcare settings.</p></p>
