<p><p>The extruded polyolefin foam market consists of three primary types: PP (polypropylene) foam, PE (polyethylene) foam, and EVA (ethylene-vinyl acetate) foam. PP foam is known for its lightweight and durable properties, making it suitable for packaging and automotive applications. PE foam, widely used for insulation and cushioning, offers excellent moisture resistance and cushioning. EVA foam combines flexibility and durability, making it popular in sports, footwear, and packaging industries. Each type caters to diverse applications based on specific performance characteristics.</p></p>
