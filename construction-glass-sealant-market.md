<p><p>The construction glass sealant market is categorized into single-component and multi-component types. Single-component sealants are ready-to-use products that cure through moisture or air exposure, offering convenience and ease of application. In contrast, multi-component sealants require mixing two or more components before application, providing superior adhesion and flexibility but necessitating more careful handling. The choice between these types depends on specific project requirements, including environmental conditions, application methods, and desired performance characteristics in sealing glass installations.</p></p>
