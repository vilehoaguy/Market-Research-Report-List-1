<p><strong>The Total Knee Arthroplasty Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed-bearing Implants</li><li>Mobile-bearing Implants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Total Knee Arthroplasty (TKA) involves two primary implant types: fixed-bearing and mobile-bearing implants. Fixed-bearing implants have a stable polyethylene insert that remains in one position, providing durability and ease of installation. Conversely, mobile-bearing implants feature a moving polyethylene component, allowing for greater articulation and potentially improved range of motion. The choice between these types depends on factors like patient activity level, surgeon preference, and specific knee conditions, influencing outcomes and longevity of the implant.</p></p>
