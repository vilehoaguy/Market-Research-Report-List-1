<p><p>The scanning spectrophotometer market is segmented into UV, Visible, and Infrared spectrophotometers, each serving distinct analytical needs. UV spectrophotometers analyze light absorption in the ultraviolet range, ideal for studying biomolecules and pharmaceuticals. Visible spectrophotometers operate in the visible light spectrum, commonly used in colorimetric analyses in environmental and food testing. Infrared spectrophotometers measure molecular vibrations, providing insights into chemical structures and functional groups, crucial for organic chemistry and industrial applications. Together, these instruments are vital for research and quality control across various industries.</p></p>
