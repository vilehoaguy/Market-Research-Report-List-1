<p><p>The dental intraoral X-ray market encompasses various types, with Bitewing X-rays and Periapical X-rays being the most prevalent. Bitewing X-rays capture the upper and lower teeth in one image, facilitating the detection of interproximal cavities and assessing tooth alignment. Periapical X-rays focus on a specific tooth or area, providing detailed views of the root structure and surrounding bone. Other types may include occlusal X-rays and panoramic images, contributing to comprehensive dental diagnosis and treatment planning.</p></p>
