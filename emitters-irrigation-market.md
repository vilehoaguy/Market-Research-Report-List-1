<p><p>The emitters irrigation market encompasses various types of systems, primarily focusing on drip and micro-irrigation methods. Drip irrigation delivers water directly to the root zone of plants through a network of tubes and emitters, ensuring efficient water usage and reduced evaporation. Micro-irrigation refers to systems that apply water in smaller quantities, utilizing micro-sprayers or subsurface drip technologies.
