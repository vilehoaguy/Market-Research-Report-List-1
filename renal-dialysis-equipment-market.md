<p><p>The renal dialysis equipment market primarily consists of two main types: hemodialysis and peritoneal dialysis. Hemodialysis involves using a machine and a dialyzer to filter blood outside the body, typically performed in clinics or at home. In contrast, peritoneal dialysis uses the patient’s abdominal cavity to filter waste by introducing a dialysis solution, offering flexibility and independence for patients. Both modalities cater to patients with chronic kidney disease, providing essential treatment options for managing renal failure.</p></p>
