<p><ul><li>Tapered Thread Type</li><li>Parallel Thread Type</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rebar connector market is segmented into three main types: tapered thread type, parallel thread type, and others. Tapered thread connectors feature a conical design that enhances load distribution and allows for smooth alignment during installation, making them ideal for varied applications. Parallel thread types have horizontal threads that provide consistent strength and are easier to manufacture, suited for standard construction needs. The "others" category includes specialized connectors designed for niche applications, incorporating innovative features or materials to meet specific engineering requirements.</p></p>
