<p><strong>The Photoinitiators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cationic Photoinitiator</li><li>Free Radical Photoinitiator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photoinitiators market is primarily divided into two types: cationic photoinitiators and free radical photoinitiators. Cationic photoinitiators initiate polymerization through a cationic mechanism, typically using UV light, and are commonly used in applications like coatings and adhesives due to their superior adhesion and chemical resistance. In contrast, free radical photoinitiators generate free radicals upon exposure to light, leading to polymerization for inks and coatings. Each type serves specific industry needs based on the desired properties of the final product.</p></p>
