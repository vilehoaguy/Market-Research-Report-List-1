<p><strong>The Power Distribution Cables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HV</li><li>MV</li><li>LV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The power distribution cables market is categorized into three main types based on voltage levels: High Voltage (HV), Medium Voltage (MV), and Low Voltage (LV). HV cables, typically over 33 kV, are used for long-distance transmission, connecting power plants to substations. MV cables, ranging from 1 kV to 33 kV, distribute electricity within urban and industrial areas. LV cables, below 1 kV, deliver power to residential and commercial buildings, ensuring safe and efficient electricity supply at the end-user level.</p></p>
