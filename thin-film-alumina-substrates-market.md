<p><p>The Thin Film Alumina Substrates market is primarily categorized into two types: multi-layer and single-layer substrates. Single-layer substrates consist of a single thin layer of alumina, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness for various applications. In contrast, multi-layer substrates incorporate multiple layers of alumina, providing enhanced performance through better thermal and electrical properties. This allows for improved signal integrity and heat dissipation, making multi-layer substrates ideal for high-frequency and high-power applications in electronics and optoelectronics.</p></p>
