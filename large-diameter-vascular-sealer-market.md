<p><strong>The Large Diameter Vascular Sealer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Sealer</li><li>Active Sealer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The large diameter vascular sealer market comprises two main types: passive sealers and active sealers. Passive sealers rely on mechanical closure methods, often utilizing sutures or clips to secure vascular connections, minimizing the risk of leakage. In contrast, active sealers employ advanced technologies like radiofrequency or laser energy to achieve hemostasis through tissue bonding and thermal coagulation, offering quicker sealing and reduced procedural times. Both types serve critical roles in vascular surgery, enhancing patient outcomes and procedural efficiency.</p></p>
