<p><p>The Carbon Fiber Reinforced Thermoplastic (CFRTP) composites market can be categorized into two primary types: Polyacrylonitrile (PAN)-based CFRTP and pitch-based CFRTP. PAN-based CFRTP, known for its superior strength-to-weight ratio and excellent thermal resistance, is widely used in automotive and aerospace applications. In contrast, pitch-based CFRTP offers a cost-effective alternative with good mechanical properties, making it suitable for various industrial uses. Both types leverage the advantages of thermoplastics, such as rapid processing and recyclability.</p></p>
