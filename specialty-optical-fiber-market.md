<p><strong>The Specialty Optical Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multimode PM Fiber</li><li>Single-Mode PM Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The specialty optical fiber market features two primary types: Multimode Polarization-Maintaining (PM) Fiber and Single-Mode PM Fiber. Multimode PM Fiber is designed for short-distance data transmission, allowing multiple light modes to travel simultaneously, which is ideal for applications requiring high bandwidth in confined spaces. In contrast, Single-Mode PM Fiber supports long-distance communication by allowing only one light mode to reduce signal loss and enhance performance, making it suitable for telecommunication and high-speed data transfer applications.</p></p>
