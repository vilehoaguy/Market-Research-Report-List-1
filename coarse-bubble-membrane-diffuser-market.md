<p><p>The coarse bubble membrane diffuser market includes two main types: disc diffusers and tube diffusers. Discs, typically mounted on a base, disperse air in the form of large bubbles, enhancing aerobic processes in wastewater treatment. Tube diffusers, elongated and flexible, release air through perforations along their length, providing even distribution and reduced clogging. Both types improve oxygen transfer efficiency and are crucial for enhancing the performance of aeration systems in various environmental applications, particularly in wastewater management.</p></p>
