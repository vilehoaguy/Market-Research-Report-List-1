They are often used in aerospace and infrastructure applications. On the other hand, organic nanocoatings, which utilize polymers and coatings derived from organic compounds, offer flexibility and ease of application, making them suitable for various surfaces in automotive and consumer goods. Both types enhance performance and longevity against ice buildup.</p></p>
