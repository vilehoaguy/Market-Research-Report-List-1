<p><strong>The Liquid Level Switches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Type</li><li>Non-Contact Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid level switches market is categorized into two main types: contact and non-contact types. Contact type switches directly interact with the liquid, utilizing mechanisms like float sensors or conductive probes to detect levels. In contrast, non-contact type switches use technologies such as ultrasonic, capacitive, or radar to measure liquid levels without physical contact. Each type serves distinct applications, with contact types often used in simpler systems while non-contact types are preferred for more advanced or corrosive environments.</p></p>
