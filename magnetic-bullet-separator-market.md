<p><ul><li>Electromagnet</li><li>Permanent Magnet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Magnetic Bullet Separator market is categorized into two main types: Electromagnet and Permanent Magnet separators. Electromagnetic separators utilize electric currents to create a magnetic field, offering adjustable strength and versatility for various applications. They are suitable for processing non-ferrous materials and can be turned off when not in use. Permanent magnet separators, on the other hand, rely on fixed magnets, providing a constant magnetic field without the need for power. They are typically used in continuous operations and offer reliability with lower maintenance costs.</p></p>
