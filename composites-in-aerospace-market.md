<p><p>The aerospace composites market consists of various materials, including glass, carbon/graphite, ceramic, and aramid. Glass composites are lightweight and cost-effective, suitable for non-structural applications. Carbon/graphite composites offer high strength-to-weight ratios and excellent rigidity, making them ideal for critical structural components. Ceramic composites provide thermal resistance and are used in high-temperature applications. Aramid composites, known for their toughness and impact resistance, are often found in aircraft interiors and protective gear. Each type plays a vital role in advancing aerospace technology.</p></p>
