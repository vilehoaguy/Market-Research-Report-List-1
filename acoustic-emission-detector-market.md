<p><p>The Acoustic Emission Detector market is divided into two main types: Single Channel and Multi Channel systems. Single Channel detectors focus on monitoring a single location or source of acoustic emissions, making them cost-effective for specific applications. In contrast, Multi Channel detectors can monitor multiple locations simultaneously, providing a comprehensive overview of the monitored structure's health. This allows for more detailed analysis and faster detection of issues, making Multi Channel systems ideal for large and complex facilities where multiple sources of emissions need oversight.</p></p>
