<p><ul><li>Multielectrode Ablation Catheters</li><li>Single Point Ablation Catheters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cardiac ablation catheters market is primarily categorized into multielectrode and single point ablation catheters. Multielectrode ablation catheters feature multiple electrodes, enabling simultaneous treatment of multiple sites, which enhances efficiency and reduces procedure time. Conversely, single point ablation catheters focus on delivering energy to a specific location, allowing for targeted ablation. Both types are essential for treating arrhythmias, offering distinct advantages depending on the clinical scenario, including precision, versatility, and procedural effectiveness in cardiac interventions.</p></p>
