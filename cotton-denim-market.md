<p><p>The cotton denim market can be categorized into three main types: light, medium, and heavy denim. Light denim, typically weighing less than 8 ounces per square yard, is soft and breathable, making it ideal for summer garments. Medium denim, ranging from 8 to 12 ounces, offers versatility for everyday wear, suitable for casual and formal styles. Heavy denim, over 12 ounces, is durable and robust, often used in workwear and high-quality jeans, providing enhanced strength and longevity.</p></p>
