<p><p>The fridge water filters market includes various types, primarily focusing on Granular Activated Carbon (GAC) filters and Carbon Block filters. GAC filters contain loose granules that effectively remove impurities and enhance water taste, although they can have lower contaminant reduction capabilities. Carbon Block filters, on the other hand, consist of tightly packed carbon that provides superior filtration efficiency, capturing smaller particles and a broader range of contaminants. Other filters in the market may encompass various technology combinations to target specific impurities.</p></p>
