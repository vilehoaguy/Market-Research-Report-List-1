<p><p>Commercial DJ turntables are primarily categorized into three types: direct-drive, belt-drive, and others. Direct-drive turntables are preferred for their quick start-up times and durability, making them ideal for professional performances. Belt-drive models are characterized by their quieter operation and are commonly utilized by casual DJs and audiophiles for home use. Other types may include hybrid or DJ controllers that integrate digital technology, catering to modern DJs who blend traditional and electronic music techniques. Each type serves unique needs in the market.</p></p>
